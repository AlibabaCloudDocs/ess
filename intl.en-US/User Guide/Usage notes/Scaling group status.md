# Scaling group status {#concept_mx5_2cn_qfb .concept}

This topic introduces the status of the scaling group.

A scaling group has three statuses: Active, Inactive, and Deleting.

|Status|API indicator|
|:-----|:------------|
|Creating|Inactive|
|Created|Inactive|
|Enabling|Inactive|
|Running|Active|
|Disabling|Inactive|
|Stopped|Inactive|
|Deleting|Deleting|
